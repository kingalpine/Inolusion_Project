Patient Records

Track patients and their personal information as well as medication, allergies, and status.

Therapist Records

Track therapists and their personal information as well as their status.

Therapy Sessions

Track therapy sessions and their information such as date, start time, end time, and medication.

Therapy Notes

Track therapy notes for a therapy session such as inverention and Speech CPT, therapy SOAP notes, and discharge plan.

Incidents

Track patient and therapist incidents that occured in a therapy session as well as date and time of an incident.

Reports

Generate reports used by the business based off Patient Records, Therapist Records, Therapy Sessions, Therapy Notes, and Incidents.
