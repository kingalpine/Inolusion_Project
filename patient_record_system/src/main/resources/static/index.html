Patient Record System has the functionality to view, add, modify, and delete patient records, employee records, therapy sessions, therapy progress notes, and incidents. PRS is also capable of generating reports such as a therapy progress note, active patient personal information, therapy sessions conducted by month, and incidents by patient.
